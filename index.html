Stonecrest Golf Course

StoneCrest Golf Course is a unique facility owned and operated by the City of Prestonsburg, located in Southeastern Kentucky. The course sits on 700 acres of mountain top land and offers amenities such as a pro shop, club repairs, practice green, driving range, clubhouse and lounge. PAR 72 YARDS 6475 RATING 70.8 SLOPE 124

Yatesville Lake Course

Yatesville Lake Golf Course, part of Yatesville Lake State Park, is located in the scenic eastern Kentucky foothills near Louisa, Kentucky. Architects Arthur Hills/Steve Forrest design highlights the rugged beauty of the region in creating a golfing masterpiece, with manicured fairways and immaculate greens. You will not only be treated to a wonderful, challenging test of golf but you will be able to enjoy an abundant array of wildlife, dramatic tee to green elevation changes and scenic views which will leave you awestruck.
